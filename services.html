Through our thought-provoking interactive material, real-world examples and visual aids, we provide a glimpse into symptoms and behaviours experienced by many autists.
Just as every person is unique – so too is their disability. There is power in words, and as such, we have included an introduction to people-first language with a focus on its importance in the workplace. Our workshop will dispel common misconceptions, promote inclusivity and spark a confidence in your students and employees in the use of people-first language.
In workplace settings, autistic people often have their own unique brand of specialization. They can work with an excellence, which can be very, very beneficial for businesses. Given the right environment, autists can offer qualities such as hyper focus, speedy work (without the loss of quality that that phrase might usually imply), independence, tendency to follow orders to the letter and irreplaceably unique outlooks on situations. However, autistic people do need support in the workplace. To get the absolute most out of your autistic employees, certain accommodations must be made, and we aim to promote these accommodations.

Our range of workshops include:
Autism Awareness for Businesses
In alignment with the UNCRPD, this awareness talk gives employers and employees a fundamental understanding of Autism while showing how to better interact with and support autistic people in society.
Secondary School Autism Workshops
We provide an interactive workshop to secondary schools around Ireland. Our workshop will give students and teachers a fundamental understanding of Autism Awareness.
