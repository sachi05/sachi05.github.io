About Us

Understanding Le Chéile is a social enterprise led by a dedicated team of students of National University of Ireland, Galway. Our aim is to teach a fundamental awareness to Businesses and Second Level Education Institutes around Ireland. With our interactive and thought-provoking workshops, students and employees alike will leave with a better understanding of autism and comfortably know how to interact and support autistic people in the workplace and society.

Understanding Autism

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurological disorder comprised of numerous characteristics.
These include inattention, sensory sensitivities, obsessive-compulsive tendencies, narrow ranges of interests, distinct repetitive behaviours, poor executive function, and difficulties in socialising, connecting and empathising with others.The severity of each characteristic varies in each individual as autism is a spectrum.

Services

Our services include secondary school autism workshops and autism awareness talks for businesses. We believe in reducing inequalities and our workshops in schools and businesses do exactly that. Our workshop, with its people-first language, will dispel common misconceptions, promote inclusivity and spark a confidence in students and employees. Understanding Le Chéile is the perfect way to encourage, create and nurture an environment founded on respect, inclusiveness and collaboration.
