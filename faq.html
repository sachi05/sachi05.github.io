FAQ

How can the Understanding Le Chéile workshop be beneficial to you?

Our aim is to raise awareness about autism, a condition that affects 1 in 59 Irish people. You may know someone who has autism, but often know very little about the condition itself, and the challenges it encompasses. With our workshops you will no longer fear causing offence and will instead desire to know more.

What can you expect to learn from the workshop?

“If you’ve met one person with autism, you’ve met one person with autism”-Dr. Stephen Shore. It would be impossible to cover all aspects of autism – there is no one-size fits all! We aim to instill confidence in everyone that following our workshop you will have an insight into this condition, based wholly on scientific findings and raw, personal experiences.

Why Choose the ‘Le Chéile’ team?

All ‘Le Chéile’ team members have undertaken a comprehensive module on Autism Awareness Training, certified and conducted by Positive Behavioral Specialist, Brian McDonald (R.N.I.D., M.A.). Paul Byrne is a 19- year old autist studying Arts in NUI Galway and he is our content advisor. With both the clinical and personal aspects of autism incorporated into one concise workshop, Understanding Le Chéile is a must for all Schools and Businesses.
